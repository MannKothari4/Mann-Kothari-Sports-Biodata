Mann Kothari 🤸

Hi, I am Mann Kothari, 18 years old, national-level men's artistic gymnast from India. Height: 167 cm, Weight: 57 kg. Began gymnastics at the age of 6 at Alpha Gymnastics, Singapore, where early international invitational competitions in Hong Kong and Thailand, with multiple medals, sparked a deep interest in the sport. Trained for 9 years at PTKS Gymnastics, Mumbai under coaches Vishal Katakdound and Shailendra Lad. Currently training at South Essex Gymnastics Club, UK under coach Anthony Wise for the past 2 years.

Selected as a Khelo India athlete, through the Indian Talent Identification Program.

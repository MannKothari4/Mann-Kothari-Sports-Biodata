🌏Vision and mission:

This journey is bigger than me. My mission is to help build the gymnastics community in India — from a niche group to a nationwide movement. I want to make gymnastics a sport that more people can relate to, enjoy, and understand while watching. It deserves to be in the spotlight, and so do the athletes who dedicate their lives to it.

By performing, mentoring, and promoting the sport, I hope to play a role in taking Indian gymnastics to the global stage — both in performance and popularity
